If your dream is to explore vibrant underwater life, Catalina Island is your destination. Unlike other tours, we focus on the spectacular coral reefs surrounding this Caribbean jewel. Dive into turquoise waters to witness a kaleidoscope of tropical fish before relaxing on pristine white sands.

**Duration:** 12 hours

**Included:**
• Convenient hotel pickup and drop-off
• Visit to a local artisan souvenir shop
• Scenic boat transfer to the island (approx. 25 mins)
• Refreshments served on board
• Beachfront buffet lunch and drinks
• Professional snorkeling gear (mask, snorkel, and buoyancy aid)
