Go beyond the resort walls and see the real Dominican Republic. Aboard our open-air 4x4 trucks, you'll visit local schools, mountain plantations, and family-run ranches. This is a journey of flavors, culture, and stunning landscapes perfect for all ages.

**Duration:** 8 hours

**Included:**
• Safari truck transportation
• Educational tours of coffee, cacao, and tobacco farms with tastings
• Visit to the Basilica of Higüey and the local market
• Traditional ranch lunch and a swim in the Maimón River
• Scenic horseback riding through tropical trails
• Beach time at Macao Beach
• Open bar and buffet lunch
