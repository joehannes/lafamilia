Get ready to get muddy! This off-road journey takes you through the heart of the Dominican countryside. Drive your own buggy through rugged trails, explore the hidden Macao Cave cenote, and finish with a refreshing dip at the world-famous Macao Beach.

**Included:**
• Hotel pickup and drop-off
• All-terrain buggy and safety gear
• Swimming in a natural underground cave
• Visit to a traditional plantation (cacao, coffee, tobacco)
• Relaxation time at Macao Beach
