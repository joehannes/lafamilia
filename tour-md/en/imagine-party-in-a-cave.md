Descend into the earth for a night you'll never forget. Imagine is a massive natural cavern transformed into a state-of-the-art nightclub. With different rooms playing various genres and acoustics that only a cave can provide, it's the most unique party venue in Punta Cana.

**Duration:** 4 hours

**Included:**
• Entrance to the cave nightclub
• Round-trip hotel shuttle
• Open bar (all night) depending on ticket type
• Live DJ sets and entertainment
