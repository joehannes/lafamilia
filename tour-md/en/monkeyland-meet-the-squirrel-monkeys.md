Get up close and personal with the most charming residents of the Dominican jungle. Visit Monkeyland, where friendly squirrel monkeys will jump on your shoulders and eat from your hands. A heart-warming experience perfect for nature lovers and families.

**Duration:** 4 hours

**Included:**
• Hotel pickup and drop-off
• Guided tour of the monkey sanctuary
• Educational talk on monkey habits and habitat
• Tastings of local organic coffee, chocolate, and fruit
