Discover the Dominican Republic's most Instagrammable peak. Perched 300 meters above the northeast coast, Montaña Redonda offers 360-degree panoramic views that will take your breath away. Swing over the edge of the world and enjoy a traditional meal at the summit.

**Duration:** 4-5 hours

**Included:**
• Hotel pickup and drop-off
• Thrilling Safari Truck ascent to the summit
• Access to the famous 'flying' broomsticks and hammocks
• Traditional Dominican lunch with a view
• Free time for photography
• Visit to a local Macao shop and beach
