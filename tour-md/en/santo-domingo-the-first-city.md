Step back in time in the oldest city in the Americas. Walk the cobblestone streets of the Colonial Zone, visit the palace of Christopher Columbus's son, and marvel at the first Cathedral ever built in the New World. A deep dive into the soul and history of the Dominican Republic.

**Duration:** 10 hours

**Included:**
• Private air-conditioned transportation
• Entrance fees to all major monuments and 'Los Tres Ojos' cave park
• Guided tours of Alcázar de Colón and the National Cathedral
• Visits to the Chocolate Museum and jewelry workshops (Amber & Larimar)
• Traditional Dominican lunch and drinks
• Full liability insurance
