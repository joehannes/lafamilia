Unleash your inner explorer at Scape Park. From flying over the trees on zip lines to swimming in the hidden turquoise waters of the 'Hoyo Azul' cenote, this park offers a perfect blend of adrenaline and natural beauty.

**Duration:** 4-5 hours

**Included:**
• Round-trip hotel transportation
• All-day admission to park attractions
• Bilingual guides to assist throughout the park
