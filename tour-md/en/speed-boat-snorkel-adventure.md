Be the captain of your own adventure! Pilot your own speedboat across the turquoise waters of Bávaro. This high-speed thrill is paired with a peaceful snorkeling session in a protected coral reef teeming with tropical fish.

**Included:**
• Hourly hotel transfers for maximum flexibility
• Private speedboat (1 per couple)
• Professional snorkeling gear
• Stop at the Bávaro Natural Pool for a refreshing ocean dip
• Expert guide in a lead boat
