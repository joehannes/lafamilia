Soar through the lush canopy of the Anamuya Mountain on one of the Caribbean's longest zip line circuits. With 12 lines spanning across 18 platforms, you'll experience a pure adrenaline rush while surrounded by breathtaking tropical nature.

**Duration:** 5 hours

**Included:**
• Safari truck hotel transfers
• ACCT-certified safety equipment and instructors
• Full circuit of 12 zip lines
• Refreshing fruit and drinks
